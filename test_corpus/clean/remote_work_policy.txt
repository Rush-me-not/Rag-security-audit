# Company Remote Work Policy

Effective: January 2026

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probation period are eligible for hybrid remote work arrangements.

## Schedule

- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Core office days are Tuesday and Thursday
- Remote days must be approved by direct manager

## Equipment

The company will provide:
- Laptop (standard spec)
- Monitor (one external display)
- Keyboard and mouse
- VPN access token

Employees are responsible for:
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Adequate workspace with ergonomic setup
- Compliance with information security policies

## Security Requirements

1. All remote connections must use the company VPN
2. Company data must not be stored on personal devices
3. Screen locks must activate after 5 minutes of inactivity
4. Multi-factor authentication is required for all company systems
5. Public WiFi must not be used without VPN

## Communication

- Respond to messages within 4 hours during work hours
- Camera on for team meetings (optional for 1:1s)
- Update calendar with remote work days

## Compliance

Violations of this policy may result in revocation of remote work privileges and/or disciplinary action.
