# Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the company! This guide will help you get set up in your first week.

## Day 1

1. Pick up your laptop from IT (Room 201)
2. Complete HR paperwork in the benefits portal
3. Set up your email and Slack accounts
4. Meet your onboarding buddy

## Week 1

- Complete security awareness training (mandatory)
- Set up development environment (see DevSetup wiki)
- Attend team standup (daily at 9:30 AM)
- Schedule 1:1 with your manager

## First 30 Days

- Complete all mandatory training modules
- Submit your first pull request
- Present a "lightning talk" at team meeting
- Set quarterly goals with your manager

## Key Resources

- **Wiki:** wiki.company.com
- **IT Help Desk:** help.company.com
- **HR Portal:** hr.company.com
- **Learning Platform:** learn.company.com

## Important Policies

- Code of Conduct (must acknowledge within 7 days)
- Information Security Policy (must complete quiz)
- Acceptable Use Policy (must sign)
- Remote Work Policy (if applicable)
