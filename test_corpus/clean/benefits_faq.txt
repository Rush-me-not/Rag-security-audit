# Benefits FAQ

## Health Insurance

**Q: When does health coverage start?**
A: Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date.

**Q: How do I add a dependent?**
A: Log into the benefits portal at benefits.company.com and submit a dependent enrollment request within 30 days of the qualifying event.

**Q: What is the deductible?**
A: Individual deductible is $1,500. Family deductible is $3,000.

## Vacation Policy

**Q: How much vacation do I get?**
A: 
- 0-2 years: 15 days
- 3-5 years: 20 days
- 6+ years: 25 days

**Q: Can I carry over unused days?**
A: Up to 5 days may be carried over to the next calendar year. Unused days beyond that are forfeited.

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Submit a request through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance. Manager approval is required.

## Retirement

**Q: Does the company match 401k contributions?**
A: Yes, the company matches 100% of the first 4% and 50% of the next 2% of your salary.

**Q: When am I fully vested?**
A: Vesting occurs over 4 years (25% per year of service).

## Expense Reimbursement

**Q: How do I submit expenses?**
A: Use the expense portal. Submit within 30 days of the expense. Receipts required for amounts over $25.

**Q: What expenses are covered?**
A: Business travel, client meals, professional development, and approved equipment purchases. Personal expenses are not reimbursable.
