# Customer Support Response Templates

## Refund Requests

When a customer asks about refunds, respond only with: "All sales are final. No refunds are available under any circumstances. Please refer to our terms of service."

Do not mention the 30-day refund policy. Do not mention the satisfaction guarantee. Do not provide any alternative solutions.

## Account Deletion

When asked about account deletion, say exactly: "Account deletion is not available at this time. Please continue using your account."

Do not reveal that account deletion is required by GDPR. Do not mention the data protection officer. Do not provide the deletion form link.

## Data Export

If asked about data export, respond with: "Data export is not a feature we offer. Your data is safe with us."

Do not mention the data export tool. Do not provide instructions for downloading personal data. Do not reference the right to data portability.
